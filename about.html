About WEMCO Engineers

A legacy of excellence in facility management since 1996.

Our Journey

Founded in 1996, WEMCO Engineers started as a small firm specializing in HVAC, electrical, and plumbing services. Over the past 28 years, we have grown into a leading facility management company in Pakistan, serving a wide range of industries with innovative solutions.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive services including energy audits, system maintenance, janitorial services, fire safety, MEP services, and skill development programs. Our solutions are tailored to meet the needs of corporate, government, and public sectors.

Who We Serve

  • Corporate: Offices, banks, embassies, and multinational companies.
  • Financial: Banks, insurance firms, and financial institutions.
  • Government: Defense, Treasury, IT, and public sector organizations.
  • Public: Educational institutions, hospitals, and community centers.

Certifications

  • SECP (Security Exchange Commission of Pakistan)
  • PEC (Pakistan Engineering Council)
  • FBR (Federal Board of Revenue)
  • IESSI (ICT Social Security Institution)
  • EOBI (Employees' Old-Age Benefits Institution)
  • ICCI (Islamabad Chamber of Commerce and Industry)

Our History

From humble beginnings in Islamabad, we’ve expanded our operations nationwide, completing over 5,000 projects and building a team of 300+ professionals dedicated to excellence.

Our Team

Our team comprises experienced engineers, technicians, and management professionals who bring expertise and passion to every project, ensuring top-notch service delivery.
